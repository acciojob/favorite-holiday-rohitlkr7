Chhath Puja


Chhath Puja is a very important festival celebrated mainly in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Nepal. During this festival, people worship the Sun God and pray for the health and prosperity of their families. Devotees observe fasting and perform rituals near rivers or ponds during sunrise and sunset.

Top 5 Attractions
  1. Sunrise Arghya – Devotees offer prayers to the rising sun.
  2. Sunset Arghya – A beautiful ritual performed in the evening.
  3. River Ghats – Devotees gather near rivers for prayers.
  4. Traditional Songs – Special Chhath songs are sung during the festival.
  5. Decorated Baskets – Fruits and sweets are offered in bamboo baskets.
Must-Try Festival Foods
  • Thekua – A traditional sweet made from wheat flour and jaggery.
  • Kheer – A sweet rice pudding prepared during the festival.
  • Seasonal Fruits – Like banana and sugarcane offered to the Sun God.
  • Rasiya – Sweet jaggery rice dish.
  • Dry Fruits – Offered as part of the puja rituals.
